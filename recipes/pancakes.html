Pancakes

How to make pancakes

this is a beginner-friendly pancake recipe

Ingredients

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • milk and butter
  • egg

Steps

  1. sift dry ingredients together
  2. make a well, add wet ingredients and stir to combine
  3. scoop batter onto a hot griddle or pan
  4. cook for two or three minutes then flip
  5. continue cooking until brown on both sides
