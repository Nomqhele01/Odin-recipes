Chicken Stew

How to make Chicken Stew

this is a beginner-friendly chicken stew recipe that will be paired with rice

Ingredients

  • chicken pieces
  • salt and pepper
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 potato

Steps

  1. pat the chicken dry and season
  2. brown the chicken
  3. saute the vegetables
  4. mix veggies and chicken in one pot then add water
