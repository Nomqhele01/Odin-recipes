Lasagna

How to make Lasagna

This is a beginner-friendly lasagna recipe designed by a beginner coder

Ingredients

Bolognese

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • red wine
  • favourite seasonings

White sauce

  • butter
  • flour
  • milk
  • cheese

Assembling

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. cook bolognese
  2. smear a small amount of bolognese onto the base
  3. line lasagna sheets onto base
  4. layer 1: top with meat sauce with small amount of white sauce
  5. Layer 2: lay more lasagna sheets then top with more meat sauce and white sauce
  6. layer 3: repeat until top of ceramic plate
  7. topping: cover with lasagna sheets, pour remaining white sauce and sprinkle top with cheese
